About

Practical learning support from Solvanta Consulting.

Solvanta Consulting is being built as a free learning platform focused on simple courses, practical exercises, and verifiable certificates.

Purpose

Solvanta Consulting aims to provide free, practical learning resources for people who want to build everyday professional skills. The focus is on clear explanations, useful exercises, and certificates that can be verified online when issued.

What Solvanta Consulting provides

The website is designed around three main areas: courses, practice exercises, and certificate verification. Courses and exercises will be added gradually, with priority given to practical topics such as AI prompting, workplace communication, basic Excel, and interview readiness.

Simple courses

Short learning resources focused on useful skills rather than unnecessary complexity.

Practical exercises

Practice tasks designed to help learners apply concepts in realistic, everyday situations.

Verifiable certificates

Certificates issued by Solvanta Consulting may include a unique certificate ID and public verification link.

Privacy-first approach

Solvanta Consulting is designed to collect only the information required for the feature being used. The platform does not require user accounts for the current public website. Practice exercises are intended to run without storing user responses, unless a future feature clearly states otherwise.

Certificate position

Certificates issued by Solvanta Consulting confirm completion of a Solvanta Consulting learning activity or assessment. They are not licences, government approvals, or accredited qualifications unless explicitly stated by a recognised authority.
