Certificates

Certificates issued by Solvanta Consulting.

Solvanta Consulting may issue certificates for eligible courses and assessments. Certificates are designed to be clear, verifiable, and limited to the learning activity completed.

Certificate types

C

Certificate of Completion

A Certificate of Completion may be issued when a learner completes the required course content or learning activity, where applicable.

A

Certificate of Assessment

A Certificate of Assessment may be issued when a learner completes an assessment and meets the required pass score. The default pass score is 70%.

Verification

Each issued certificate can be checked online.

Certificates may include a unique certificate ID and a QR code linking to the public verification page. The verification page displays certificate status and basic certificate details.

Verify a certificate Read certificate policy

What verification may show

Certificate status

Valid, revoked, deleted, or not found.

Learner name

The full name provided for the certificate.

Course details

Course name, certificate type, issue date, and score if applicable.

Important note

Certificates are not licences or accredited qualifications.

Certificates issued by Solvanta Consulting confirm completion of a Solvanta Consulting learning activity or assessment. They should not be described as a government-approved qualification, licence, or accredited credential unless separately stated by a recognised authority.
