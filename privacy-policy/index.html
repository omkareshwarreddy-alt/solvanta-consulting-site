Privacy Policy

How Solvanta Consulting handles information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected through this website, why it is used, and how users can request deletion of their information.

Last updated: 06 June 2026

1. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Solvanta Consulting website and related website features, including contact messages, certificate verification, and certificate support requests.

2. Information collected

Solvanta Consulting is designed to collect only the information needed for the feature being used.

Contact and feedback messages

If you contact Solvanta Consulting through the website or by email, the following information may be collected:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content
  • Reason for contact

Certificate records

If a certificate is issued by Solvanta Consulting, a minimal certificate record may be stored for verification purposes. This may include:

  • Certificate ID
  • Full name provided for the certificate
  • Course name
  • Certificate type
  • Score, if applicable
  • Issue date
  • Certificate status

Practice exercises

Practice exercises are intended to run without storing learner responses, unless a specific feature clearly states otherwise and asks for consent.

3. Information not collected by default

The current website does not require user accounts. Solvanta Consulting does not collect resume data, interview responses, phone numbers, addresses, or learner profiles by default.

4. Purpose of using information

Information may be used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to contact or support messages
  • Issuing and verifying certificates
  • Handling certificate correction, deletion, or support requests
  • Maintaining website security and reliability
  • Complying with applicable legal or operational requirements

5. Public certificate verification

Certificates issued by Solvanta Consulting may be publicly verified using a certificate ID or QR code. The public verification page may show the certificate status, learner name, course name, certificate type, issue date, score if applicable, certificate ID, and issuer name.

Email addresses and private contact details are not displayed on the public certificate verification page.

6. Data retention

Contact messages are retained only as long as needed to respond and maintain related communication history, unless deletion is requested earlier.

Certificate records may be retained for verification purposes unless deletion is requested by the certificate holder or the record is otherwise removed.

7. Data deletion requests

Users may request deletion of contact messages or certificate records. If a certificate record is deleted, the certificate may no longer be available for public verification.

Deletion requests can be submitted through the contact page or by emailing Solvanta Consulting.

8. Children and minors

Solvanta Consulting does not intentionally target children. Where a user is under 18, they should use the website only with permission from a parent or guardian, especially when requesting a certificate or submitting contact information.

9. Security

Solvanta Consulting takes reasonable steps to protect information collected through the website. However, no website or online service can guarantee complete security.

10. Third-party services

The website may use services such as GitHub Pages, Google Workspace tools, Google Apps Script, Google Sheets, Google Drive, and email services to host the website, process contact messages, generate certificates, or maintain certificate records.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised update date.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or deletion requests, contact:

helpdesk@solvantaconsulting.com
