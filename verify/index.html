Certificate verification

Verify a Solvanta Consulting certificate.

Enter the certificate ID exactly as shown on the certificate. Certificate verification will display the public record associated with certificates issued by Solvanta Consulting.

Certificate lookup

The verification system will be connected to the Solvanta Consulting certificate records. This page is prepared for certificate ID lookup.

Status

Certificate result

What verification may show

A valid certificate record may show the certificate status, participant name, course name, certificate type, issue date, score if applicable, certificate ID, and issuer name.

Email addresses and private contact details are not displayed on the public verification page.

Important

Certificate records may be deleted on request.

If a certificate holder requests deletion of their certificate record, the certificate may no longer be available for public verification. This does not necessarily mean the certificate was invalid; it may mean the record was removed following a deletion request.
